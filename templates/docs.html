PricePro Documentation

How PricePro uses data and reinforcement learning to suggest smart prices for your products.

Interface and visuals are kept intentionally minimal in pure black and white.

System Overview

PricePro is a smart pricing assistant that suggests an optimal selling price for your product using cost price, stock age, stock quantity, live competitor prices and search trend signals.

Key Features

Data

Competitor Analysis

Collects product listings from Amazon and Flipkart to see how similar items are priced, along with ratings and basic popularity indicators.

Signals

Trend Insights

Uses Google Trends (or an uploaded CSV) to approximate demand over time and generate a simple demand factor for pricing decisions.

Model

RL-Based Pricing

A trained SAC (Soft Actor–Critic) model reads cost, stock, age, and demand factor and outputs a recommended price; if unavailable, a rule‑based fallback is used.

How to Use PricePro

  1. Enter product name, cost price, stock age, and stock quantity on the home page.
  2. Select the most relevant category from the dropdown.
  3. Click “Get Suggested Price” and wait for the loader to finish.
  4. Review the Final Suggested Price, adjustments, competitors, and charts on the result page.
  5. Use “Download Report” to export a CSV summary if needed.

Pricing Logic

The final price is built from several components:

  • Base Price: A starting value derived from cost .
  • Trend Adjustment: Small upward/downward change depending on demand signal.
  • Stock Adjustment: Larger stock pushes price slightly down; low stock can keep it higher.
  • Age Adjustment: Older inventory gets discounted more to avoid dead stock.
  • SAC Output: When the RL model runs successfully, its suggested price becomes the final recommendation.

All visuals across the app (forms, cards, charts) follow the same black‑and‑white theme for a clean, distraction‑free interface.
